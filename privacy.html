Privacy Policy for SwiftRead

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SwiftRead ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application and web services.

1. Information We Collect

SwiftRead is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.

2. Reading Content

When you upload files (PDF, EPUB, TXT) to SwiftRead, the processing happens locally on your device. Your book content is never uploaded to our servers or shared with any third parties.

3. Permissions

To provide our features, the app may request the following permissions:

  • Storage/Files: To allow you to select and read books from your device.
  • Internet: To provide web-based features and fetch necessary libraries.

4. Data Storage

All app data, including your reading history, statistics, and book metadata, is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data.

5. Third-Party Services

We use standard libraries (like PDF.js or JSZip) to parse files. These libraries operate entirely on your device. We do not use third-party tracking or analytics services.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
