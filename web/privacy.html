Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 20, 2026

Swift Reader ("we", "our", or "us") is dedicated to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our application.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Swift Reader is designed as a local-first application. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data, reading history, or uploaded files to any external servers owned by us.

  • Reading Material: Books (EPUB, PDF) and text you upload are processed entirely on your device.
  • Library Data: Your reading progress, bookmarks, and settings are stored locally on your device using your browser's or phone's local storage capabilities.

2. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions to function:

  • Storage / File Access: Required to open and read the book files you select from your device.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party analytics services that track your behavior. The app is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may collect standard server access logs (IP address, user agent) for security and maintenance purposes.

4. Children's Privacy

Our service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
